About

REDWOLF ESPORTS

Indonesia Esports Organization




Founded in June 2019, REDWOLF ESPORTS was born by 3 Apex Legends players who started from a community and finally agreed to build an esports. In June, REDWOLF began to develop into other games such as the popular Free Fire Battleground game and then to the highly successful mobile game, Mobile Legends. Now REDWOLF has developed into four game divisions including FREEFIRE, APEX LEGENDS, MLBB and PUBGM by getting some of the best talents not only in Indonesia but also regionally.
